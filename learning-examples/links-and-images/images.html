Images

This page covers comon image formats, but you may also be looking for linksand useful extras

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos.But do not allow for transparent pixels

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations. But they have a limited colour palette

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons. but can be larger in size

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. Use them wherever you can.They are excellent for scaling up or down on responsive websites. But if image contains a lot of text the file size can be large
